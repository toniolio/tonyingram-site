Summary

PhD neuroscientist and AI product leader who founded and led a neurotechnology startup from concept to clinical pilots at leading institutions (MGH, TIRR Memorial Hermann), raising $5M+ and filing three patents. Currently leads AI initiatives at Clarivate on a drug discovery intelligence platform used by top global pharmaceutical companies: spanning problem definition, evaluation harness design, and shipping production features at enterprise scale. Experience across hospitals, academia, startups, and global enterprise, with a track record of building cross-functional teams and delivering measurable outcomes. Comfortable partnering with regulatory, reimbursement, market-access, and privacy experts to ship safely in healthcare. Through-line: turning technically difficult ideas into reliable systems with clear clinical and business impact.

Executive Highlights

  • Enterprise AI: At Clarivate, advanced an AI agent to enterprise early access (Q4 2025) and built a rigorous evaluation harness; also shipped the first LLM-based search feature and reduced problematic searches by 25%.
  • Neurotech founder to clinical pilots: Founded and led a startup from “0 to 1” – concept to working device and software, including clinical pilots at MGH and TIRR Memorial Hermann; raised $5M+ and filed three patents.
  • Team leadership at two scales: Built a multidisciplinary team from scratch to ship a novel device; now drives cross-functional execution at enterprise scale across engineering, data science, UX, and go-to-market.
  • Research record: 15+ peer-reviewed publications in neuroscience and rehabilitation; PhD thesis recognized with a national academic excellence award.
  • Clinical grounding: Former licensed physiotherapist specializing in neurorehabilitation; translates clinical realities and safety constraints into product decisions.
  • Execution style: Known for clear problem framing, measurable definitions of “good” and “done”, and closing the loop from discovery to shipped product and adoption.
